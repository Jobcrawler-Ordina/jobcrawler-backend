Titel

Mechanical Engineer

Organisatie

Hightech manufacturer of printers.

Achtergrond opdracht

We are looking for a Mechanical Engineer who is passionate about precision mechanisms and development of high-tech machines. Someone who is able to make optimal choices between disciplines in order to solve issues or set up new designs for a good performance at acceptable cost. The Mechanical Engineer will add value to our products by developing and engineering units and components in a multidisciplinary environment.


The job


In our product development projects at R&amp;D you are responsible and focusing on mechanical topics like media handling systems, high precision frames, linear and rotational drive systems, print heads, ink fixation units, and more.


Together with the stakeholders (Manufacturing, Service, Purchasing, Quality Assurance) you specify and agree on requirements based on project demands. Together with the designers and architects you come up with technical designs and concepts that meet these objectives, properly balancing time, quality, functionality and costs (QFTC)


Within the project you collaborate with colleagues from other disciplines. You review your ideas and jointly develop smart creative solutions that meet the overall project objectives. You test and review your model based design(s) by building prototypes to proof the feasibility of the routes you have identified.


In this role there are plenty of opportunities to further develop yourself, for example to Lead Mechanical Engineer, Mechanical Designer, etc.


Responsibilities

  • Designing mechanical components and units in NX CAD/CAE
  • Specify, realize and verify the mechanical design requirements
  • Units and parts documentation (Drawings, Instructions, Requirements, etc.)
  • Tolerance decompositions in units
  • Reviews with vendors
  • Perform effectively in a multi-disciplinary team


Functie eisen

We are looking for someone with an eye for detail and excellent problem solving skills. A real team player that is hands-on, practical, analytical and goal oriented. Besides that, he or she needs to have:


  • Bachelor degree in mechanical engineering or similar
  • A maximum of 5 years of experience
  • Knowledge of design principles for precision mechanisms
  • Knowledge about manufacturing technologies such as Sheet Metal (demanded), Machining and Plastics
  • Experience with parametric CAD/CAE systems is plus
  • Experience in early phase engineering is a plus
  • Fluent in English, verbal and written. Profound knowledge of Dutch is a plus
